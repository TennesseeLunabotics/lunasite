Outreach

The educational outreach program is a large part of the team's activities during the year. Each event is a great opportunity for education, engineering, and STEM majors to work together in developing activities for the public and K-12 students. We hope to inspire others to learn about robotics, engineering, planetary science, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

The Tennessee Lunabotics team continually searches for opportunities to outreach to our campus community, other campuses around us, and our community here in Knoxville. If you are interested in planning an outreach event, please send us an email at lunabotics@utk.edu.
