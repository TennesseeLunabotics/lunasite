The Competition


The competition is centered around designing, building, and operating
a lunar mining robot in a simulated lunar regolith.

Teams that are successful in the first two phases of the competition
will be invited to participate in live testing of their robot at
Kennedy Space Center in Florida.

The competition consists of three phases:

- PHASE I -
DESIGN IT
Teams design the model for an autonomous/remote-controlled robot


- PHASE II -
BUILD IT
Teams assemble robot design


- PHASE III -
DIG IT
After completing all Phase II requirements, select teams will be invited to the Phase III
on-site challenge at the Kennedy Space Center (Florida).


The 2022 competition week begins on Mon May 23, 2022.
