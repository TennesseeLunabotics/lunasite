Outreach

The educational outreach program is a large part of the team's activities
during the year and a great opportunity for educators, engineers, and other
science majors to work together in developing activities and events for the public
and K-12 students. We hope to inspire others to learn about robotics, engineering,
planetary science, and interdisciplinary collaboration.


The Tennessee Lunabotics team continually searches for opportunities to
outreach to our campus community, other campuses around us, and our
community here in Knoxville.
