FAQ


What is the competition?

→ Our end goal for the competition is to create a robot that could navigate and mine on the moon. This will be tested during competition week, where teams will gather at the Kennedy Space Center to test their robots on an obstacle course that simulates the moon’s surface.
→ For more information, see our Competitions page.
