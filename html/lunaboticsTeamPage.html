The Team

Lunabotics is a student-deigned organization of students from all sorts of backgrounds.
Due to the range of the competition, we recruit students from various majors, and
encourage those interested to join.

The organization is divided into five major teams.

Each team is lead by our team executives.



Design

Before anything can be built, ur design team is tasked with modeling possible
compositions for each mechanical element of the robot.

Adry Lain (Design Lead)



Fabrication

Fabrication focuses on the mechanical assembly of all pre-designed elements.

Mike Fox (Fabrication Lead)



Electrical

ELectrical implements the varied automated needs of the robot through networking.

Joey Motto (Electrical Lead)



Programming

For autonomy and control systems, Programming codes customized functions that allow
controller input and automation.

Matthew Bowlby (Programming Lead)



Business

Tennessee Lunabotics could not supplement any of its teams without Business. Acting
as our financial backbone, Business manages funding, donation, and sponsorships.

Erin Dunn (Business Lead)
