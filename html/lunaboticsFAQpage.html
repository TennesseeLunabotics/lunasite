FAQ

What is the competition?

→ Our end goal for the competition is to create a robot that could navigate
 and mine on the moon. This will be tested during competition
 week, where teams will gather at the Kennedy Space Center to test
 their robots on an obstacle course that simulates the moon’s surface.
→ For more information, see: Competitions

Why should I join?

→ Lunabotics is a fun way to get involved on-campus and learn more about robotics along the way.
 There’s a place for everyone, even if you don’t have much experience.
 Building, programming, writing, advocating, researching and more
 -- there are many things you can do!
→ Your involvement may be something to put on your resume, too — especially if we
 do well in the competition.
